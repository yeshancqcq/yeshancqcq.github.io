Outreach

I have been doing outreach and popsci writings in China for several years. Currently I am a part-time writer and editor in collaboration with several Chinese magazines, publishing houses and online platforms. Topics of my outreach works are mainly on geoscience and GIS but also include human geography, historical geography and others. I have hold live or recorded outreach talks on some online platforms on topics of geology and geography in China. I have also been invited to give talks in communities and local primary/secondary schools to popularize geoscience among general public in America.

Music
