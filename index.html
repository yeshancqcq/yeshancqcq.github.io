Shan Ye | Geoscientist & GIS Analyst

Currently, I am a graduate student in the Department of Geoscience at University of Wisconsin-Madison focusing on climate change and paleoclimatology. My advisors are Dr. Shaun Marcott and Dr. Shanan Peters. I am mainly working on the EarthCube project as a research assistant. Before coming to Wisconsin, I received an M.S. degree in Geosciences at University of Tulsa and a B.S. degree in Earth and Environmental Sciences at University of Michigan. I also had two years of intensive trainings and coursework in GIS at Penn State University.
